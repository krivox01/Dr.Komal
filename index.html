FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions our patients ask.

Is root canal treatment painful?

Modern root canal treatment is virtually painless. With advanced local anesthesia and gentle techniques, most patients feel little to no discomfort. The procedure actually relieves the severe pain caused by infected or damaged tooth pulp.

How long does teeth whitening take?

Professional in-clinic teeth whitening typically takes 60–90 minutes. You can see your teeth become 5–8 shades whiter in a single session. Results typically last 1–3 years with proper maintenance.

How often should I visit a dentist?

We recommend visiting a dentist every 6 months for a routine check-up and professional cleaning. Regular visits help detect issues early, saving you time, money, and discomfort in the long run.

Are braces suitable for adults?

Absolutely! Orthodontic treatment works for patients of all ages. We offer traditional metal braces, ceramic braces, and clear invisible aligners. Adults often prefer clear aligners for their discreet appearance.
